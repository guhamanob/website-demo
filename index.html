Welcome to BSA-PSU



Bangladesh Student Association (BSA) at The Pennsylvania State University, University Park is the official organization that represents the Bangladeshi students of this university. The association aims to provide a common platform for the Bangladeshi community at the Penn State University. At the same time it is working relentlessly to uphold the indegeneous culture of Bangladesh.

Another objective of this association is to maintain a rapport with other cultural and ethnic groups. As a part of this, BSA-PSU organizes and participates in different cultural programs both on and off campus to represent and disseminate Bangladeshi culture.

BSA-PSU is also working for the welfare of the society. It is taking part in different community services patronized by the university.
